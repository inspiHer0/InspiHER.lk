ABOUT US

What is InspiHer ?

InspiHER{Tech} V3.0 is an event series we are organizing, aimed at empowering girls in technology and engineering. It follows two previous iterations: InspiHER{Tech} V1.0 and V2.0. The event consists of multiple competition phases, with a focus on skills development.

Who is this program for ?

InspiHER{Tech} V3.0 is designed for girls and young women(undergraduates) interested in technology, engineering, and STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) fields. The goal of the program is to inspire and empower participants by providing them with hands-on experiences, challenges, and competitions that help develop their technical skills and creativity.

What are the benefits of participating?

Participating in InspiHER{Tech} V3.0 offers young women the chance to develop hands-on technical skills, boost their confidence, and solve real-world problems through challenges like coding, design, and hackathons.

What can you develop ?

InspiHER{Tech} V3.0 helps participants develop valuable technical skills like programming, engineering, and problem-solving, while fostering creativity and innovation. It also enhances soft skills such as teamwork, leadership, and communication, boosting confidence through project presentations and competitions. Additionally, participants gain networking opportunities with mentors and industry professionals, helping them build connections and prepare for future success in STEM.

InspiHER(CAD Mastery)

18-22 DEC 2024

A hands-on AutoCAD training focusing on mechanical automation and product design. Participants will submit a project to showcase their skills.

InspiHER(CAD challenge)

23-31 DEC 2024

Participants will apply their AutoCAD skills to develop a creative and technically accurate product design project. Submissions will be evaluated based on creativity, practicality, and alignment with the given theme.

InspiHER(ByteBattle)

12 JAN 2025

An engaging online quiz designed to test participants’ understanding of key IT concepts, including programming fundamentals, cybersecurity, data structures, and cloud computing. This phase encourages quick thinking and technical proficiency.

InspiHER(HackArena)

18 JAN 2025

A competitive hackathon where teams will collaborate to develop real-world solutions using programming languages like Python, Java, C, and C++. This phase focuses on problem-solving, teamwork, and technical innovation.

Closing Ceremony

18 JAN 2025

The ceremony will celebrate participants' achievements with winner announcements, awards, and inspiring speeches, marking the successful conclusion of InspiHER{Tech} V3.0.

ROADMAP

GUIDELINE

ELIGIBILITY

  • The competition is open to female undergraduates in STEM and IT programs from any university.
  • Participants should have a passion for technology and innovation.

TEAM COMPOSITION AND REGISTRATION GUIDELINS


  1. Team Structure

    • Each team must consist of exactly 3 female undergraduate students.
    • A team leader must be designated during registration.
    • No changes to team members are allowed after registration.

  2. Unique Registration Requirement

    • Participants can only be registered with one team.
    • Any participant found to be part of multiple teams will lead to the disqualification of all associated teams.

  3. Registration Deadline

    • Ensure your team completes the registration process before the specified deadline. Late registrations will not be accepted.

  4. WhatsApp Group and Mentor Assignment

    • Once registered, all team members will be added to a WhatsApp group.
    • Each team will be assigned a dedicated mentor to provide guidance and support throughout the competition.
